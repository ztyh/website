Probability and Statistical Inference, 9thEd.Hogg, Tanis & Zimmerman, 2015 required.

Objective:
This course provides an introduction to mathematical statistics, covering the fundamentals of statistical inference. The topics covered will include: sufficiency, point estimation, interval estimation, hypothesis testing, analysis of variance, chi-square test, likelihood ratio test, regression analysis and Bayesian estimation.
