On our hearing

With one ear, you cannot know anything about the source of the sound.
Two ears make a line so you can discern the left and right of the source.
Three ears make a plane so you can further discren the front and back.
Four ears make a tetrahedron so you can further discern the up and down.
I feel that I can discern the location of the source of sound in all three axis.
This is either due to my skin and bone detecting sound,
or some secondary information such as vision.
