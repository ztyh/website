Proposal for a new input method

Chinese characters are recognized and read faster, but it is harder to write.
But computers remove the difficulty of input.
You only need to know how to pronounce the character.
You type it's pronunciation/spelling and select your desired character.
As long as you can recognize characters, you can perform this step.
All languages can take advantage of the speed of pictorial recognition.
An input method can allow selection of Chinese characters from English input.
I would not think it too absurd for China or Japan to use tax money on this.
